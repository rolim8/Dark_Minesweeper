How to Play Dark Minesweeper

Classic Mode

  • Left Click: Reveal a cell
  • Right Click: Toggle flag on a cell
  • Goal: Reveal all non-mine cells without hitting any mines
  • Numbers: Count of adjacent mines (1-8)

Quantum Mode

  • Some cells exist in quantum superposition (purple glow)
  • They may or may not contain mines until you click them
  • Clicking collapses the quantum state
  • Adds strategic depth and unpredictability
